WHAT IS UNDERWATER HOCKEY?

Six versus six. On the bottom of the pool.

Two teams, snorkels, and short sticks. One puck. The goal is to push it into the opposing trough. It's fast, three-dimensional, and a workout you won't get anywhere else.

📅
When & where
Tuesdays 8:30pm and Sundays 6pm at Spectrum Leisure Centre, Guildford.
🥽
What to bring
Just swim kit and a towel. We lend you sticks, fins, mask and snorkel.
👋
Who can play
Anyone 14+ who can swim 50m. No previous experience needed at all.
