FIND US

How to Find Us, and When

Where we play, what time, and what each session is for.

SUNDAYS

Juniors and adults at the Spectrum

Juniors arrive from 5:00pm and spend about 30 minutes on snorkelling, puck and teamwork skills. After that they play games against each other or against the adults to put their skills into practice. Holiday schedule varies — check with the committee.

Adults warm up from 5:45 to 6pm, then play in the competition pool from 6pm onwards.

WEDNESDAYS (JUNE — AUGUST)

Summer at the Lido

Quick warm-up followed by a game at Guildford Lido, 7–8pm. A lovely large pool with good visibility. Come along and try it — summer months only.

WEDNESDAYS (SEPT — MAY)

Dive pit at the Spectrum

7:45–8:45pm, games in the 4m diving pool at the Spectrum. Note this is a change from the previous Tuesday Five Pit sessions.

DON'T MISS

Special events

  • Turkey Burn — the first Sunday after Christmas. Sensible game to start, four-way knockout to finish.
  • Underwater Rugby — the last dive pit session before Christmas can be a fun underwater rugby session.
  • Barbipush — see the Competitions page for the annual Lido tournament plus BBQ.
